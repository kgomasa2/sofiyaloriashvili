About

Sofiya Loriashvili is a contemporary artist working at the intersection of photography, painting and experimental visual narratives.

Her work explores the transformation of classical forms through modern technical lenses — mysticism, architectural rhythm and anatomical deconstruction — with a sophisticated use of color and texture.

Born in Ukraine, currently based between Paris and Tbilisi.
