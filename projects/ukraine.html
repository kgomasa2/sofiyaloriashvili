FROM UKRAINE

An ongoing visual letter from home — daily life, people and landscapes between war and tenderness.

Years
2022 — present
Medium
Photography & video
Status
Ongoing
Press
VICE, i-D, Libération

"From Ukraine" is the project I never planned to make. It began on 24 February 2022 and hasn't stopped since — a continuous notebook of the people, streets and small ruined kitchens that still feel like home.

Parts of the work have been published by VICE (in three separate features), i-D and Libération. The Rezavod chapter was projected at the Museum of Warsaw and at Arles "Night of the Year" in 2023.

The series is updated every few months — what you see here is the current edit.
