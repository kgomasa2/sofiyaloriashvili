Feet

A small fetish for the unnoticed — feet, ankles and the ground beneath us.

Year
2025
Medium
35mm photography
Format
Series of 32
Status
Selection on view

"Feet" looks down. Tiles, asphalt, hotel carpets, an ankle wrapped in a sock — the everyday architecture we walk past without noticing.

The series is intentionally low-stakes. It's a love letter to the small, the close, and the things that hold us up.
