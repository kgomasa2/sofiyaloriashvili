only you and me

A diary of love and isolation. Featured in Fisheye Magazine and exhibited at Festival d'Arles 2024.

Year
2024
Medium
Photography & book
Publisher
Fisheye éditions
Pages
96 — 140×210 mm

"Only you and me" is the visual diary of a relationship lived under glass — two people, one apartment, the slow folding of time during the months of confinement.

The series was first published as SUB#4 with Fisheye éditions in 2024 and exhibited the same year at Festival d'Arles, in the 10/10 selection. A signed first edition is still available in the shop.
