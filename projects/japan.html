japan

31 days in Japan — published in Dazed and exhibited at the Design Festa Gallery.

Year
2024
Medium
35mm + digital
Locations
Tokyo, Kyoto, Osaka
Published
Dazed

One month, three cities, two cameras. "Japan" is a slow walk through the country I'd been postponing for years — the tiny kissaten coffee shops, late-night izakayas, and the very specific quality of light that falls through Tokyo windows in November.

The full series was published as "31 days in Japan" by Dazed and shown at the Design Festa Gallery in Tokyo in November 2024.
