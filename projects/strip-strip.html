strip strip

A study of the gaze, performance and the body as a site of autonomy.

Year
2025
Medium
Photography & performance
Format
Series of 18
Status
Available as prints

"Strip strip" pulls apart the choreography of being looked at. Each frame is staged with the performer, never the photographer, in control of the angle, the moment, the cut.

The work is the second chapter of an ongoing inquiry into bodily autonomy and the politics of the strip — building on the merch line of the same name available in the shop.
